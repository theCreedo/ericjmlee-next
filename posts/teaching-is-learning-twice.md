Hello friends,

Greetings from Austin!

This past Sunday, I taught my first lesson for my home church in Dallas on giftings for college students!

Personally, I struggled a lot in college on discovering my giftings and knowing how to apply them, so I was blessed to have the opportunity to teach from my experience.

It was fun being able to practice Zoom public speaking (staring at a camera 80% of the time is difficult) as well as apply principles I learned from a book called “[Communicating for a Change](https://www.amazon.com/Communicating-Change-Seven-Irresistible-Communication/dp/1590525140)” (10/10 recommend if you desire to be a theological teacher or pastor), and I hope for more opportunities to teach in the midst of this quarantine! 😛

If you want to hear [how to utilize your giftings for God or learn a biblical perspective of giftings](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NdnV5O0krMc), definitely check out the lesson! (forgive the zoom quality though 😐)

* * *

The truth is, although I don’t get to formally teach often and there are not many subjects I can teach well, I love to teach.

Teaching is a great way to pass on knowledge, see others grow, and most importantly, one of the quickest ways to learn.

**In fact, I’d like to think teaching is a fast track to becoming an expert.**

![Teaching is Learning Twice](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!RvZ-!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5d1f1366-d794-4807-95e9-2ad35bb6440a_1260x886.png)

Above is shown the [four stages of competence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Four_stages_of_competence) sectioned out in quadrants. Although most people who teach effectively live in the right half of the graph, teaching can happen at almost any stage (the effectiveness is definitely lost the farther left you go).

The place most people struggle in after recognizing what they’re incompetent in is moving from:

-   Q2 to Q3 - conscious incompetence to conscious competence through learning & practicing
    
    and
    
-   Q3 to Q4 - conscious competence to unconscious competence through experience
    

For me, I believe the most effective and efficient way to go from Q2 to Q4 is through teaching as you will be able to practice and gain experience at the same time.

**You will learn to know what you’re teaching and know it well enough so that others can follow you.**

It is a humbling process though.

I remember one of the most embarrassing humbling moments of teaching happened recently when I tried to share the Gospel through a new method, the “[3 Circles Gospel](http://lifeonmissionbook.com/conversation-guide)”. (displayed below)

![Teaching is Learning Twice](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!i1DD!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9c3c7097-5c1c-4048-a083-59ea4dfdc6de_1132x852.png)

With over 20 years of Christian faith tenure through Vacation Bible Camps, missions. and even leading a life group, I thought the diagram was simple and believed teaching a new format wouldn’t take too long.

But as I went through the process, drawing out each circle and describing the events, I found myself fumbling whilst sharing in real-time about our broken world and even struggled to believe the promises I was sharing.

As fortunate as it was a practice round with a teacher, within 5 mins of attempting to share the gospel to someone who might not know anything about God, I felt exposed in my faith, learned what I took for granted, and found gaps in my understanding of the Gospel.

That experience taught me to recognize the importance of **teaching for others so I could teach myself.**

“Teaching is learning twice”, and though I feel helpless whenever I teach, I welcome the process, as they are moments I am sharpened in so I can be able to learn and teach better for the future.

Anyways, an epilogue for that embarrassing story, I now vlog a lot in order to practice and teach myself the things I’ve learned. You can see my attempt of [teaching the three circles gospel shortly after that embarrassing moment here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5zibXuciI7o&feature=youtu.be&fbclid=IwAR0lRdjD8mGzrp0rbU8N_hXfnCnn1bIV1aSqXGMnTK85cjQXO5ZDUjgY3z4). Hopefully, you can follow along what I’m sharing through the visual above, and let me know if you want to learn how to draw and teach it yourself! I will gladly teach you so you may be equipped to teach others (as well as redeem myself 😅)

With that, thanks for checking! Find spontaneous ways to keep in touch with people, seek ways to serve and teach if you can, and I'll see y'all in the next update! 😊

There are **8760** hours in a year.

Approximately **1,920** hours (excluding standard holidays and vacation time) is spent at a typical 9 to 5 job.

**2920** hours is spent asleep (if you follow the standard 8 hours a day sleep schedule, which I know most of you don’t 👀)

And the other **3920** hours is our budgeted “free time” in life.

Here you can venture into **my experiences in those 3920 hours of my life.**\*

![](https://outsidethe9to5life.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/screenshot-2024-06-07-at-2.04.23e280afpm.png?w=1024)

I know, a selfish request.

Another blog in the universe. Another voice shouting into the digital void. Why need another one?

![](https://outsidethe9to5life.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/screenshot-2024-06-07-at-2.12.28e280afpm.png?w=1024)

As an entrepreneur, engineer, and student of life, there are stories and learnings that I wish I knew beforehand, that I gleaned from other people sharing their life experiences.

Learning from the path already paved by other’s lives always helped me in figuring out my own life.

And I have a lot I’d love to share with y’all!

Some stories I’d love to share:

-   The trading card business I built that allows me to travel the US for free
-   The time I worked on a startup idea while recovering from heart surgery
-   The struggle of committing to a personal weekly newsletter for 2 years

And many more after that.

So if you’re interested, join me at my campfire.

I hope you enjoy your time here!

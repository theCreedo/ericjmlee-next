_**Savvy Idea: Set your one thing to focus on for the week so you can prioritize and make the week a win.**_

Recently, I started to implement a weekly routine to figure out one thing that would [define success](https://ericlee.substack.com/p/defining-success-reason-to-step-into) if I completed it for the week.

This routine has helped me tremendously in making progress in focusing and planning.

I find that even going through the process helps organize my tasks and helps me prioritize so that after I finish my one thing, I know what next task I should try to complete.

I didn’t come up with this on my own though.

This was inspired by the book, [The One Thing](https://www.amazon.com/ONE-Thing-Surprisingly-Extraordinary-Results/dp/1885167776), written by Gary Keller and Jay Papasan.

As someone who is trying to create more discipline in life, this method has definitely helped me measure out my energy and not take too big of a bite out of the endless tasks I have to do while being able to find satisfaction weekly when I complete the one thing.

_Questions to ponder:_ 🤔

-   _What are the many things that you have to do in your life?_
    
-   _What is the one thing that you can focus on that would help you be most successful?_

_**Savvy Idea: Seeing everything laid out like the big picture can help in making strategic decisions towards specific goals.**_

When making plans, it’s important before taking any action to see the big picture.

I’ve been recently talking with a CMO friend about wanting to start a consulting business and rebrand my newsletter and website.

In order to hash out a plan, she wanted me to take note of my goals and the ventures I was running by consolidating all the parts.

Beforehand, I had a more siloed approach in how I should take strategy.

But after having everything written out on a whiteboard, a lot of connection points revealed themselves, and I had a more clear idea of how I should take action to achieve my goals.

By seeing the bigger picture, I could act knowing the reality of circumstances, and plan steps towards the goals I had.

_Questions to ponder:_ 🤔

-   Do you have a bigger picture view of the things you’re working on and your goals?

**Savvy Idea: Change is a slow, complex process. Learn to take small steps to integrate change.**

Within the past month, I’ve started to work out early in the morning at around 5:30AM.

Several years ago, I would expect habit change to happen within a few days, but I’ve learned that habits take time to become incorporated in lifestyle.

Software products have a similar pattern. When integrating a new API or product, it always takes many moving parts and people to fully integrate software into a system.

Over the past week, I’ve been able to slowly feel more balance in my energy level.

I’ve come to learn that change is slow, but over time, things balance out within a system.

By learning to take small steps and incorporate change slowly, any change can be integrated into life.

_Questions to ponder:_ 🤔

-   _What change do you want to integrate into your life?_
    
-   _What’s realistic small steps need to be taken in order to see this change happen?_
